All about Greek Myth of Hades and Persephone!

Stemming from thoousands of years ago, the ancient Greek story of Hades and Persephone is a tale of stockholm syndrome. In it, we see the collaboration between Hades and his brother Zeus to kidnap his love from Earth and take her to the Underworld. There are different versions of this involing different characters and concepts.
